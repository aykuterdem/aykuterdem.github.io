COMP541: Deep Learning

Fall 2024

Presentations

An important part of the course includes discussions of a number of papers related to certain research topics. Each paper discussion will be led by three students:

  • Overview of the paper. Two students will be responsible for providing an overview of the paper. This part of the presentation will be graded according to this rubric.

  • Strengths of the paper. Two students will present the strengths of the paper. This part of the presentation will be graded according to this rubric.

  • Weaknesses of the paper. Two students will discuss the weaknesses of the paper. This part of the presentation will be graded according to this rubric.

Deadline: You should meet with the instructor 3-4 days before the presentation date to discuss your slides, and the presentation should be submitted by the night before the class.
