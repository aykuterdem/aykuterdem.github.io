Aykut Erdem · Koç University

The broad goal of my research is to explore better ways to understand, interpret and manipulate visual data*. My research interests span a diverse set of topics, ranging from image editing to visual saliency estimation, and to multimodal learning for integrated vision and language.

I am an Associate Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at Koç University. I’m also affiliated with KUIS AI Center. Before joining Koç University, I was with Hacettepe University, where I was one of the directors of the Computer Vision Lab. I received my PhD degree from the Computer Engineering Department of METU in 2008. During my PhD studies, I spent a summer at Virginia Tech as a visiting researcher, and a semester at MIT as a visiting scholar. I did my postdoc at Ca’Foscari University of Venice in Italy.

I'm looking for motivated MSc/PhD students. Also, don't hesitate to contact me, if you're an undergrad wanting to get into AI and has a desire to learn!
